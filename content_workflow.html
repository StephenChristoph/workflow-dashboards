Week 1: Writing Cycle

Purpose: Create foundational content across priority verticals.

Output:

  • Write long-form content pieces based on abundant ideas from your own work and audience needs.
  • Draft articles or posts for:
    • Substack
    • Medium
    • Typeshare

Outcome:

  • Schedule written content across corresponding platforms using a scheduling tool.
  • Ensure consistent tone and branding across all verticals.

Week 2: Iteration Cycle

Purpose: Adapt long-form content into short-form social media posts.

Output:

  • Produce concise written iterations of Week 1's outputs for social platforms:
    • LinkedIn posts
    • X posts and threads
    • BlueSky posts
    • Threads posts

Outcome:

  • Schedule short-form posts using social scheduling tools.
  • Tailor content to platform-specific nuances for higher engagement.

Week 3: Real-Time Feedback Cycle

Purpose: Analyze performance, audience insights, and engagement.

Output:

  • Monitor content performance metrics across all platforms (views, likes, shares, comments, etc.).
  • Actively engage with your audience by:
    • Responding to comments and messages.
    • Joining conversations around similar topics.

Outcome:

  • Document insights (e.g., audience preferences, trending themes) in Notion to guide Week 4 and future cycles.
  • Make minor adjustments to scheduled posts based on early feedback if necessary.

Week 4: Video Production Cycle

Purpose: Create video content iterations based on previous weeks' insights.

Output:

  • Batch-record video iterations of written content from Weeks 1 and 2.
  • Edit videos using AI tools to optimize for platform-specific formats.
  • Create videos for:
    • YouTube
    • YouTube Shorts
    • TikTok
    • Instagram Reels
    • LinkedIn videos
    • X posts

Outcome:

  • Schedule video content across platforms using scheduling tools.
  • Ensure videos align with audience feedback and platform best practices.

Week 5: Ideation & Review

Purpose: Refine and prioritize content ideas while reviewing performance.

Output:

  • Review engagement metrics across all platforms.
  • Actively engage with audience feedback.
  • Brainstorm future content based on:
    • Performance trends
    • Audience interactions
    • Industry trends

Outcome:

  • Identify and prioritize ideas for the next cycle.
  • Document insights to ensure continuous improvement.

Week 6: Reset & Begin

Purpose: Prepare for the next content cycle while maintaining momentum.

Key Activities:

  • Review all insights from previous cycle
  • Update content strategy
  • Prepare resources and tools
  • Set specific goals for upcoming cycle

Preparation:

  • Refresh content calendar
  • Update automation workflows
  • Review team assignments
  • Ensure all tools are ready
